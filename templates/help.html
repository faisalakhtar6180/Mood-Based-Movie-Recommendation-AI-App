MovieFindr Help Center & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about using our mood-based movie recommendation system.

How does the mood detection work?

Our system uses advanced natural language processing (NLP) to analyze the text you provide in the "Describe how you're feeling..." box. It extracts emotional cues and keywords to categorize your current mood (e.g., happy, sad, anxious, excited) and matches it to appropriate movie genres and themes.

Why should I enter my email?

Entering your email is optional, but it allows us to send the resulting movie recommendations directly to your inbox. This way, you can easily access them later or share them without needing to keep the webpage open.

What if the recommendation is completely wrong?

While our AI is highly trained, it's not perfect! If you receive a suggestion that doesn't fit your mood, try rephrasing your input with more descriptive words. The system learns over time, and your feedback helps improve future recommendations for everyone.

Do you store my personal information?

We only temporarily store the mood description and optionally your email address for the purpose of sending the recommendation. We do not store detailed personal data, ratings, or browsing history for long-term profiling.
